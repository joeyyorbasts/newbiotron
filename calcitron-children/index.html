Calcitron® Children is a innovative Calcium dietary supplement for kids. Each packet of Calcitron® Children is designed to provide essential dietary calcium with important nutritional cofactors such as Vitamins A and D in a sweetened strawberry flavored drink mix.

Calcium is an essential nutrient for the development of strong bones and healthy teeth. Adequate dietary calcium is especially important in adolescents who are in their peak bone building years. Children who do not get adequate dietary calcium from the diet (mainly from dairy products like milk, cheese and yogurt, as well as certain vegetables like broccoli and cabbage) are at increased risk of fracture. More importantly, people who don't have adequate calcium intake in their youth are at greater risk of fracture and developing osteoporosis later in life.
