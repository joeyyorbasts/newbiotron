The primary concern regarding smokers is increased oxidative stress resulting from repeated exposure to the harmful compounds contained within cigarette smoke. Over time, the cumulative effect of this exposure increases the risk of developing certain chronic diseases, such as coronary heart disease, emphysema, some types of cancer and stroke.

While the primary means by which smokers can reduce the long term risks of developing these conditions is to stop smoking, there remains a need to provide smokers nutritional support to replace those nutrients which are most commonly depleted in smokers, while simultaneously providing some measure of protection against added oxidative cellular damage.

Common multivitamin supplements typically are not prepared to address the specific needs of tobacco users. Mineratron is a unique composition based on a comprehensive review of the scientific literature to identify those micronutrients found deficient in cigarette smokers, while avoiding those compounds which may actually be harmful in smokers.

Mineratron is not intended as a smoking cessation aid nor is Mineratron intended to undo the harmful effects of prolonged use of tobacco products*.

In addition to a well-balanced diet, rich in fruit, vegetables, whole grains and lean proteins, we recommend one capsule of Mineratron daily .
