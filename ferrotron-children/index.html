Ferrotron® Children is a unique iron dietary supplement, designed to provide essential dietary iron with important nutritional cofactors in a pleasant, sweetened powder with a hint of strawberry.

Iron is an essential nutrient for proper physical development in children and plays an important role in the formation of hemoglobin – the carrier of oxygen in the blood. Children who do not get adequate dietary iron from the diet (including iron-fortified foods, leafy green vegetables and lean meats) are at increased risk of behavioral problems, irritability and fatigue.

Ferrotron® Children provides the following nutrients:

Iron 10 mg from Ferractiv®*, a unique form of iron shown to be gentle on the system. 0.44 mg Copper*, an important cofactor in iron uptake. 30 mg Vitamin C, which has also been shown to improve iron absorption. In addition, Ferrotron® Children provides 0.6 mg of Vitamin B1, B2 and B6, as well as 200 mcg folic acid, 1.2 mcg Vitamin B12 and 12 mcg Biotin. Ferrotron® Children also supplies 5 mg Zinc* and 22 mcg Molybdenum*, two important minerals.

— The powder pouch is emptied into a beverage such as fruit juice or water (4-6 oz) before drinking.

— Inactive ingredients: Dextrose, Natural flavor, Xanthan Gum, Citric Acid, Malic Acid, Natural Red Color (from fruit and vegetable concentrate), stevia (natural sweetener)

— "Ferrotron®" and "Ferractiv" are used under License. * All minerals are supplied as Biotron Amino Acid Chelates and Complexes.

NOTE: Ferrotron® Children is not for use in children under the age of four. As with any nutritional supplement, please discuss with your pediatrician before giving Ferrotron® Children to your child. Accidental overdose of Iron–containing products is a leading cause of fatal poisoning in children under 6. Keep this product out of reach of children. In case of accidental overdose, call a doctor or poison control center immediately.
